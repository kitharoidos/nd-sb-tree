Math.SternBrocotTree

Description

This module implements the algorithm for branch-wise and generation-wise construction of the n-dimensional Stern-Brocot tree due to Hakan Lennerstad as specified in "The n-dimensional Stern-Brocot tree", Blekige Institute of Technology, Research report No. 2012:04.
